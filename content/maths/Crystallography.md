### Lattice and basis = crystal

The lattice is characterized by a $R^3 \to R^3$ linear operator (for a 3D crystal), which transforms the infinite grid into the appropriate shape. Equivalently, there are three "repeat" vectors, which represent the symmetry preserving translations that characterize the lattice structure.

At each point of the lattice, you have a structure, which is then repeated periodically. This describes the crystal.
